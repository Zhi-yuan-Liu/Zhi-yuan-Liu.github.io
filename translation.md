#### 邹忌讽齐王纳谏
邹忌修八尺有余，而形貌昳丽。
With height more than 8 foots, Ji Zou is a definitely fit and beautiful man.
朝服衣冠，窥镜，谓其妻曰：我孰与城北徐公美？
In a morning, he dressed in official suits and then stared at the mirror, which made him asking his wife:"How's my beauty rather than Mr.Xu?"
其妻曰：君美甚，徐公何能及君也。
"Your honor's beauty is so huge, that Mr.Xu has no chance to follow your step." His wife said.
